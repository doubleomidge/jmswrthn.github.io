Weather Proj Initial Design

Purpose Statement

The purpose of this project is to creat a Weather site that can be used to know weather conditions for users on multiple devices from phones to their desktop in a friendly and real world apllicable maner.

Audience Description

The Audience of this site will be those seeking to plan on prepare for days spent working or playing outside, weather that is event cordinators or casual groups of teens or adults making those plans. the audience is all those who have a desire to know current or future weather and capable of using a electronic device.
